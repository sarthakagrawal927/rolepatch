# RolePatch

AI-powered resume tailoring and job-application assistant.

## What it is

- Score resume fit against a job description
- Rewrite bullets for the role
- Cover letters, company research, STAR prep

## Who it's for

Job seekers who want reviewed, role-specific application materials — not generic AI fluff.

## Agent entrypoints

- https://rolepatch.com/llms.txt
- https://rolepatch.com/api/ai
- https://rolepatch.com/index.md

Dashboard routes require auth and are not agent-indexed.
